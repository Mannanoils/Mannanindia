About Us

At our coconut oil manufacturing company, we believe that the key to living a truly enriched and celebrated life lies in the small, simple things that we do every day. And that's exactly what Mannan is all about - a premium quality coconut oil that has been carefully crafted with 33 years of experience to help you achieve a healthier and more fulfilling life.

Made with only the finest, handpicked coconuts, Mannan is a rich source of medium-chain triglycerides (MCTs) and other essential nutrients that are crucial for your overall well-being. Whether you're looking to improve your digestion, boost your energy levels, or simply enhance your skin and hair health, Mannan has got you covered.

Our unique manufacturing process ensures that every drop of Mannan is of the highest quality, with no artificial additives or preservatives. So you can be sure that you're getting nothing but the purest, most natural coconut oil on the market.

At Mannan, we're not just in the business of manufacturing coconut oil - we're in the business of enriching and celebrating life. So join us today, and discover the secret to a healthier, happier, and more fulfilling life with Mannan
