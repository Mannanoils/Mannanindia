History

Mannan

Mannan The goodness of the best Coconuts in a bottle.

Like all things of supreme quality, this too begins with the best raw materials.
Handpicked ‘Kuttiyadi Coconuts’ go through a rigorous process of selection, steaming and drying before the low water content copra is milled to produce the finest cooking medium, without any additives. Stored in climate-controlled, nitrogen-blanketed, stainless-steel casks, our products contain no chemical antioxidant or sulphur.

With a strong exposure to the coconut market as early as 1970 and with the guidance of late N.K Balan, our founder Vipin Kumar entered the coconut oil market in 1985, starting off with drying the copra and extracting the oil to export to central and eastern India. After 5 years of export business, our brand Mannan was launched with a vision to nurture a healthy generation who could benefit from the goodness of coconut and enrich their lives.
In 1998, the product Mannan has been accredited by Agmark certification from the government of India for its superior quality. Under the leadership of our founder, Mannan has come a long way from export business to entering the home market and setting an example of its own for the premium quality which is undisputed.
Mannan is India’s first-ever Agmark coconut oil which is also double filtered and has maintained the set of quality standards consistently ever since 1998!
